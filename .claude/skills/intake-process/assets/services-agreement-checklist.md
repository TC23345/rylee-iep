# Services agreement — checklist for attorney

**This is not a contract. This is a checklist of substance Rylee needs the attorney to draft into binding language.** Rylee does not draft contract clauses; she hands this list to an Illinois-licensed attorney experienced with private-practice services agreements.

## Parties

- [ ] Rylee's entity name (per `workspace/business/entity-decisions.md`)
- [ ] Client (parent/guardian) — full legal name(s)
- [ ] Student named or referenced only generically (advocate's contract is with the parent, not the student)

## Scope of services

- [ ] Each service from `workspace/business/services-fees.md` is named, with the "scope in" / "scope out" language attached
- [ ] Explicit statement: Rylee is an educational advocate, not an attorney; she does not provide legal advice or representation
- [ ] Explicit statement: Rylee does not guarantee any specific educational outcome

## Fees and payment

- [ ] Fee schedule attached or incorporated by reference
- [ ] Hourly minimum, if any (e.g., 15-minute increments)
- [ ] Retainer terms (if used)
- [ ] Invoicing cadence and method
- [ ] Payment due date
- [ ] Late fee policy
- [ ] Action on non-payment (suspension of services, etc.)

## Cancellation and termination

- [ ] Client cancellation notice (24 hr? 48 hr?)
- [ ] Rylee cancellation notice (illness, conflict)
- [ ] Cancellation fee policy
- [ ] Termination by either party (notice period)
- [ ] Refund of unused retainer on termination

## Confidentiality and records

- [ ] Confidentiality obligations of Rylee
- [ ] Acknowledgment that Rylee handles FERPA-protected records (educational records) — speak to attorney about whether contract needs explicit FERPA language given Rylee is not a school official
- [ ] Records retention policy (how long Rylee keeps client files post-termination)
- [ ] Client's right to copies of their own records

## Communication and conflicts

- [ ] Permitted communication channels
- [ ] Response time expectations
- [ ] Conflicts of interest disclosure (e.g., Rylee's current school employer)
- [ ] Process for raising disputes between Rylee and client

## Liability and indemnity

- [ ] Limitation of liability clause — attorney's call on what's enforceable in IL
- [ ] Indemnification (if any)
- [ ] Professional liability insurance acknowledgment

## Governing law

- [ ] Illinois
- [ ] Venue (county)
- [ ] Mediation / arbitration before litigation, if desired
